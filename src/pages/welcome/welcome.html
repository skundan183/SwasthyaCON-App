Welcome To SwasthyaCON 2019

Dear Fellow Colleagues and Friends,

It is with great pleasure that we invite you to participate in the 1st International Conference of Metabolic disease; SwasthyaCON 2019 to be held in Ahmedabad, India on 12th & 13th Jan 2019. The Conference is jointly organized by All India Institute of Diabetes Research (AIIDR) & Swasthya Diabetes Care and supported by International Diabetes Federation and D FOOT International.

The overall theme of the Conference is “A journey from Traditional to Clinical”. It will be a platform for participants to discuss the current situation, challenges and advancement, relating to the complications of Diabetes.

In tune with the theme, new data & evidence based and successfully applicable advance therapy options (Clinical) will be presented while also including the time tested (Traditional) alternatives. The approach of this enlightening event is “Learn, Share & Teach”.

A lot has been spoken about this great city of Ahmedabad with a glorious history of over 600 years & is the first Indian city to get UNESCO recognition as a World Heritage city. The state of Gujarat is globally known for its hospitality & culture, which you will experience as our honorable guests.

Along with you, we are expecting academicians from across India & abroad, and endeavour to provide an excellent blend of National & International faculties to enrich your knowledge and experience.

Awaiting you for this academia at the World Heritage City of Ahmedabad
